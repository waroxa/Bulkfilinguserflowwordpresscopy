## üìñ WELCOME & OVERVIEW

### What is NYLTA Bulk Filing?

The NYLTA Bulk Filing System allows you to file New York LLC Transparency Act (NYLTA) reports for multiple clients simultaneously. Instead of filing each client individually, you can process 5, 10, 50, or even 100+ filings in one streamlined submission.

### Who Should Use This System?

‚úÖ **Accounting Firms** managing multiple business clients  
‚úÖ **Law Firms** handling corporate compliance for clients  
‚úÖ **Compliance Professionals** working with portfolio companies  
‚úÖ **Corporate Service Providers** managing entity filings  

### Key Benefits

- **Save Time:** Process multiple filings in minutes, not hours
- **Reduce Costs:** Tiered pricing means lower per-filing costs for volume
- **Early Bird Savings:** Submit early and save 10% on your entire order
- **One Payment:** Single transaction for all your clients
- **Instant Confirmation:** Receive PDF receipts for each filing immediately
- **Dashboard Tracking:** Monitor all your filings in one place

## üîÄ UNDERSTANDING FILING TYPES

Every client will follow **one of two paths**:

### Path 1: Beneficial Ownership Disclosure

**When to Use:**
- Client has beneficial owners with 25%+ ownership
- Client has individuals with substantial control
- Client is a standard operating LLC

**What You'll Need:**
- Company Applicant information (person who filed formation documents)
- Beneficial Owner details (name, DOB, address, ID, ownership %)
- Up to 4 beneficial owners per client (contact us if you need more)

**Typical Examples:**
- Family-owned LLCs
- Partnership LLCs
- Single-member LLCs with owner having substantial control

---

### Path 2: Exemption Attestation

**When to Use:**
- Client qualifies for a specific exemption under federal law
- Client is a large operating company
- Client is a regulated financial entity
- Client is inactive

**What You'll Need:**
- Exemption category selection from approved list
- Optional Company Applicant information (if you have it on file)

**Important:** Company Applicant data is **OPTIONAL** for exemption filings, but **REQUIRED** for disclosure filings.

**Approved Exemption Categories:**
- Bank
- Credit Union
- Insurance Company
- Securities broker/dealer
- Investment company/adviser
- Accounting firm
- Public utility
- Large operating company
- Tax-exempt entity
- Inactive entity
- And more (see full list in wizard)

**Compliance Note:** This system records your attestation that the client qualifies for the selected exemption. You are responsible for determining exemption eligibility based on federal BOI regulations.

## üìä CSV TEMPLATE GUIDE

### Option 1: Download Pre-Filled Template

**Recommended for most users**

1. In Step 1 of the wizard, click **"Download CSV Template (With Examples)"**
2. Open the file in Excel or Google Sheets
3. Review the example rows to understand the format
4. Delete the example rows
5. Add your client data
6. Save as CSV
7. Upload in the wizard

### Template Structure

The CSV has **57 columns** organized in sections:

**Columns A-H:** Basic Company Information  
**Column I-J:** Exemption Data (for exemption filings only)  
**Columns K-R:** Company Applicant 1  
**Columns S-Z:** Company Applicant 2  
**Columns AA-AH:** Beneficial Owner 1  
**Columns AI-AP:** Beneficial Owner 2  
**Columns AQ-AX:** Beneficial Owner 3  
**Columns AY-BF:** Beneficial Owner 4  

### Required vs Optional Fields

**Always Required (Columns A-H):**
- LLC Legal Name
- NY DOS ID Number
- Federal EIN
- Formation Date
- Country of Formation
- State/Province of Formation
- Contact Email
- Client Filing Type ("disclosure" or "exemption")

**Required for Exemption Filings:**
- Exemption Category (Column I)
- Exemption Explanation will auto-fill

**Required for Disclosure Filings:**
- Company Applicant 1 (Columns K-R) - all fields
- Beneficial Owner 1 (Columns AA-AH) - all fields
- Additional owners as applicable

### Data Validation Tips

‚úÖ **Dates:** Use format YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2024-01-15)  
‚úÖ **Filing Type:** Must be exactly "disclosure" or "exemption" (lowercase)  
‚úÖ **Exemption Category:** Must match approved categories exactly  
‚úÖ **Phone Numbers:** (123) 456-7890 format recommended  
‚úÖ **EIN:** Format as 12-3456789  
‚úÖ **Ownership %:** Enter as number without % sign (e.g., 50 for 50%)  

‚ùå **Common Mistakes:**
- Extra spaces before/after data
- Using "Exempt" instead of "exemption"
- Missing required fields
- Invalid dates
- Percentage signs in ownership percentage field

## ‚ùì COMMON QUESTIONS

### General Questions

**Q: How many clients can I file in one bulk submission?**  
A: There's no upper limit! We've processed submissions with 500+ filings. The more you file, the lower your per-filing cost.

**Q: Can I mix disclosure and exemption filings in one submission?**  
A: Yes! Each client can have a different filing type. The system handles both paths in the same submission.

**Q: What if I make a mistake in my CSV?**  
A: The system validates your data and shows you specific errors before you can submit. Fix the errors in your CSV and re-upload.

**Q: Can I save my progress and come back later?**  
A: Yes! Your data is automatically saved as you go through the wizard. You can close your browser and return later.

**Q: Do I need to create separate submissions for different filing types?**  
A: No! You can include both disclosure and exemption clients in a single bulk submission.

### Exemption-Specific Questions

**Q: Who determines if my client qualifies for an exemption?**  
A: You (the filer) are responsible for determining exemption eligibility based on federal BOI regulations. This system records your attestation.

**Q: Is Company Applicant data required for exemption filings?**  
A: No, it's optional for exemptions. If you have it on file, you can include it, but it's not required.

**Q: What if my client's exemption category isn't listed?**  
A: Contact us at support@nylta.com. We update our categories based on federal guidance.

### Disclosure-Specific Questions

**Q: What if my client has more than 4 beneficial owners?**  
A: Contact us at support@nylta.com. We can accommodate additional owners for your specific submission.

**Q: What counts as "substantial control"?**  
A: Per federal BOI regulations, this includes senior officers, decision-makers, or those with authority to make important decisions. Consult federal guidance or legal counsel.

**Q: Do I need to include percentages for substantial control owners who own less than 25%?**  
A: Yes, enter their actual ownership percentage even if it's less than 25%.

### Payment Questions

**Q: When will my payment be charged?**  
A: Payment is authorized during submission but not charged until your filings are reviewed and approved (typically 1-2 business days).

**Q: What if I need to cancel after submitting?**  
A: Contact us immediately. If we haven't started processing, we can cancel and void the authorization.

**Q: Can I get a refund if filings are rejected?**  
A: Yes! If your submission is rejected due to data errors, no payment is processed. You can correct and resubmit.

**Q: Do you offer payment terms for large volumes?**  
A: For submissions over $10,000, contact us about NET-30 payment terms.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What file format should I use for CSV uploads?**  
A: Save your file as .csv (comma-separated values). Both Excel and Google Sheets can export to this format.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**  
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Chrome is recommended for best performance.

**Q: Can I access the system on mobile?**  
A: Yes, but we recommend using a desktop/laptop for CSV uploads and data entry.

**Q: What if the upload fails?**  
A: Check your CSV for formatting errors. Ensure it's saved as .csv (not .xlsx). Try a smaller batch if the file is very large.

## üîß TROUBLESHOOTING

### CSV Upload Issues

**Problem:** "Invalid file format"  
**Solution:** Make sure your file is saved as .csv, not .xlsx or .xls. In Excel: File ‚Üí Save As ‚Üí CSV (Comma delimited).

**Problem:** "Missing required fields"  
**Solution:** Check that columns A-H have data for every row. Scroll right to ensure all required fields for the filing type are complete.

**Problem:** "Invalid date format"  
**Solution:** Use YYYY-MM-DD format (e.g., 2024-01-15). In Excel, format cells as Text before entering dates to prevent auto-formatting.

**Problem:** "Invalid filing type"  
**Solution:** Column H must contain exactly "disclosure" or "exemption" (lowercase, no extra spaces).

### Validation Errors

**Problem:** "Exemption category required for exemption filing type"  
**Solution:** For clients with filingType = "exemption", Column I must have a valid exemption category.

**Problem:** "Company Applicant 1 required for disclosure filing"  
**Solution:** For clients with filingType = "disclosure", Columns K-R must all be filled out.

**Problem:** "Beneficial Owner 1 required for disclosure filing"  
**Solution:** For clients with filingType = "disclosure", Columns AA-AH must all be filled out.

**Problem:** "Invalid ownership percentage"  
**Solution:** Ownership % must be a number between 0-100 without the % symbol.

### Payment Issues

**Problem:** "Invalid routing number"  
**Solution:** Verify your 9-digit routing number is correct. Check with your bank.

**Problem:** "Account numbers don't match"  
**Solution:** Carefully re-enter your account number in both fields. They must match exactly.

**Problem:** "Payment authorization failed"  
**Solution:** Verify your bank account details. Contact your bank to ensure ACH debits are allowed.

### Login & Access Issues

**Problem:** "Firm profile incomplete"  
**Solution:** You must complete all firm profile fields before accessing the bulk filing wizard. Go to Settings ‚Üí Firm Profile.

**Problem:** "Forgot password"  
**Solution:** Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen. Check your email for reset link.

**Problem:** "Account locked"  
**Solution:** After multiple failed login attempts, wait 30 minutes or contact support@nylta.com.

## üìù APPENDIX: FIELD DEFINITIONS

### Company Information Fields

**LLC Legal Name:** The exact legal name as registered with NY Department of State  
**NY DOS ID Number:** 7-digit identification number assigned by NY DOS  
**Federal EIN:** Employer Identification Number (XX-XXXXXXX format)  
**Formation Date:** Date the LLC was formed/registered  
**Country of Formation:** Country where entity was formed  
**State/Province:** State or province where entity was formed  
**Contact Email:** Primary contact email for this LLC  
**ZIP Code:** US ZIP code (for US entities)

### Person Information Fields

**Full Name:** Complete legal name (First Middle Last)  
**Date of Birth:** Format: YYYY-MM-DD  
**Full Address:** Complete street address, city, state, ZIP, country  
**ID Type:** State Issued Drivers License, U.S. Passport, Foreign Passport, State/local/tribal-issued ID  
**ID Number:** Full ID number (securely stored and encrypted)  
**Issuing Country:** Country that issued the ID  
**Issuing State:** State that issued the ID (if applicable)  
**Role:** For Company Applicants - their role in filing  
**Ownership Percentage:** For Beneficial Owners - ownership % (number without % sign)  
**Address Type:** For Beneficial Owners - Residential or Business
